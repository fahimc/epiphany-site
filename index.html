What is Epiphany?

Epiphany is an Ethereum token that can be used as a currency and you can trade with it.

Decentralized

The currency cannot be controlled by a government or coporation but the control lies with the community and network.

Secure

The Ethereum network is built with security in mind. Every transaction is encrypted.

Anonymous

Though every transaction is open to the public, you do not know the identity of the people making those transactions.

Fast

Ethereum based transactions are mouch faster than Bitcoin.
